What we do

Translating brain science into action.

innerscript turns evidence from neuroscience into practical mental health tools for young people. We work with educators, schools, and clinicians to make brain science useful in everyday life.

Brain science in schools

Workshops and curricula that teach students how the brain handles stress, emotion, and focus, grounded in neuroscience.

Evidence-based toolkits

Practical strategies for anxiety, OCD, PTSD, and everyday coping, built from research and reviewed by clinicians.

Awareness and leadership

Programs that help students understand mental health and take the lead as peer supporters and advocates in their own schools.
