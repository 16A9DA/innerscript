About

You don't have to face it alone.

InnerScript is a youth mental health NGO. We take what brain science knows about stress, anxiety, and coping and turn it into toolkits, workshops, and peer support that young people can actually use.

Struggling right now?

If you are in crisis, please reach out to a local emergency line or a trusted adult immediately. This community offers peer support, not a substitute for professional care.
