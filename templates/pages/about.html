About

Mental health knowledge made practical.

InnerScript is an evidence-based mental health education organization. We transform research in psychology and neuroscience into practical, accessible resources that help people better understand the mind and apply that knowledge in everyday life.

Struggling right now?

If you are in crisis, please reach out to a local emergency line or a trusted adult immediately. InnerScript provides educational information, not a substitute for professional care.
